Web Coding and Design Challenge

Design Challenge


For the design challenge, we were given spaghetti and tape, and challenged to make the tallest structure that could support a marshmellow.

After a brief planning session, the team got to work, starting with a good pyramid base, and building off of that. Unfortunatly, we didn't plan much past the bottom half of our tower, and the top half could barely support the marshmellow

If we were to do this again, I would more carefully plan the tower, and make sure it was not topheavy
