For the stickers, I started with this horrible design, then used CorelDraw X7 and fitted it to a star shape.

After I had set up the cut lines, I sent them to the machine, which printed and cut them out.


Decals


For the vinyl decals, I took this batman beyond silhouette, made it solid black, and removed te white background.

After I had done that, the rest was easy. The vinyl cutter neatly cut them out, and all that was left was to remove the unwanted parts of the decal and apply them.

Week 2:CAD and 3D Printing

Pi Box


For the Pi Box, I started by taking measurments of the actual raspberry pi. After I had those, I went into Fusion 360 and started modeling

First I made the bottom of the box, the extruded the walls.

After adding a roof for the box, I cut holes in the sides for the various ports on the pi.

Finally, I cut the box into two pieces, and seperated them.
